About Us

Genesys Now is a dynamic, progressive and innovative software development company based on the island of New Providence, The Bahamas. Started in August 2021, our mission is to drive, enhance and increase business success through the creation of bespoke business software solutions that maximise efficiency, increase profitability and continuously improve the customer experience.
It is our ultimate desire to become the company that is top of mind for software development and integration services in The Bahamas, the surrounding region and then the world.

Our core values that set us apart include our:

Quality of Work

Our focus is on excellence, innovation and out of the box thinking

Attention to Detail

We sweat the small stuff. It is the small stuff that can set a project apart and make the different.

Timeliness of Delivery

Finishing every project on time or before time is what we aim to do.

Effective Communication

We use intuitive and effective communication with our team and our clients to get answers and make decisions.

Honesty

We work responsibly and with integrity and strong work ethics

Positive Work Environment

Our culture focuses on hard work, collaboration and fun

Competitive Pricing and Value for Money

We offer super competitive prices that are often lower than our competitors. We also ensure that when our customers spend their money, they are happy with every cent that is spent.
